# NASA Space Place: The Cartoonish Cosmos

## Product Mission
Enable upper-elementary-aged kids (ages 8-12) to master space and Earth science through a digital environment that feels like a Saturday morning cartoon. The platform prioritizes "fun-first" learning via games, activities, and short-form video.

## Core Pillars
1. **Cartoonish & Playful**: Everything is a character or a toy. No "serious" science vibes.
2. **Cute & Approachable**: Low stakes, friendly feedback, and "squishy" UI.
3. **Breathable Discovery**: Anti-clutter. Each activity gets its own space to shine.

## Key Features
- **Cosmic Games**: Interactive learning challenges.
- **Hands-on Lab**: DIY activity guides.
- **Quick Bites**: Engaging short videos.
- **Knowledge Capsules**: Easy-to-read articles with cartoon illustrations.

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Kids (8-12) looking for fun online play.
- **Voice**: Cartoonish, playful, cute.
- **Anti-Vibe**: Not "bluish", not "AI-slick", not "crowded".
